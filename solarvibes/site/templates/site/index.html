Solarvibes develops Agrimodule Smart System, a network of smart sensors driven by powerful AI based crop intelligence and farm management software to aid farmers across the globe with instant access to soil health, crop status and automate the farm to achieve high crop productivity and high profit margins.

What we do?


Addressing the problems of low crop productivity, high crop failure rate, drastically increasing cost of farm operations, at Solarvibes we have developed the Agrimodule Smart System. AgriModule is a solar powered smart sensor governed by robust Smart Crop Intelligence (SCI) software. This device can be installed in any farm and controlled remotely. AgriModule's integrated helps farmers plan, manage, operate and apply lean farming and best practices in agriculture.Â Agrimodule can form a network of sensors depending on the size of the field. It can be monitored and controlled at the touch of your fingertips using our mobile application in any desired language.

Smart Crop Intelligence: The most intelligent farm assistant farmers will ever hire. The best agriculture cultivation practices and knowledge at the hand of your fingertips. It makes precise calculations based on real time and historical data which farmers can use to implement best farming practices without disturbing the soils integral structure.
