What we do?

At Solarvibes we focus on providing state of the art precision farming solutions and adequate tools necessary for farmers in emerging countries. Addressing the problems of low crop productivity, high crop failure rate, drastically increasing cost of crop failures, we have developed the Agrimodule Smart System. AgriModule is a solar powered smart sensor governed by robust Smart Crop Intelligence (SCI) software. This device can be installed in any farm and controlled remotely. AgriModule's integrated helps farmers plan, manage, operate and apply lean farming and best practices in agriculture.Â Agrimodule can form a network of sensors depending on the size of the field. It can be monitored and controlled at the touch of your fingertips using our mobile application and web application.
