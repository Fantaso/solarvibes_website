What we do?

Addressing the problems of low crop productivity, high crop failure rate, drastically increasing cost of farm operations, at Solarvibes we have developed the Agrimodule Smart System. AgriModule is a solar powered smart sensor governed by robust Smart Crop Intelligence (SCI) software. This device can be installed in any farm and controlled remotely.
