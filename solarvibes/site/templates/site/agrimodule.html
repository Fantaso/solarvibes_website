About Agrimodule

Agrimodule is a solar-powered device that sits on any farm or field and monitors the plants and soil conditions continuously 24/7.

Agrimodule comes with a pool of sensors careful chosen to monitor most important variables that affect the health and growth of the plants. It allows other sensors to wirelessly interconnect to it to create an accurate mapping network and control your pump remotely.

Agrimodule's monitoring data and the in-built Crop Intelligence help farmers plan the entire cultivation cycle and assists them every step of the way. Farmers can manage their entire farm operations remotely and efficiently using our mobile application.
